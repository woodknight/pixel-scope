PixelScope – Fast Pixel-Level Image Viewer

PixelScope is a lightweight, cross-platform pixel-level image viewer for exact image inspection, crisp nearest-neighbor zoom, source-based pixel value readout, RAW Bayer analysis, DNG and TIFF viewing, histogram overlays, image statistics, and metadata review.
